Our Purpose

Student Finance Tracker was built with the understanding that managing money can be challenging for students. Whether you're budgeting for textbooks, tracking daily expenses, or saving for future goals, this app provides the tools you need to stay financially organized.
With features like regex-powered search, visual spending analytics, and customizable budget caps, we aim to make financial management simple, intuitive, and accessible for students everywhere.
